The Problem

Attribution Breaks When AI Mediates the Sale

GA4, last-click models, and legacy affiliate platforms were not built for a world where AI assistants guide purchase decisions. The result: broken payouts, lost trust, and wasted spend.

Last-Click Lies

Last-click attribution assigns 100% of credit to the final touchpoint. Affiliates and creators who actually influenced the buyer get nothing. You optimize for the wrong signal.

AI Blind Spots

When ChatGPT recommends a product or an AI assistant compares options, there is no click trail. Traditional tools cannot see the influence. The attribution gap grows with every AI-mediated journey.

Commission Disputes

Inaccurate attribution leads to wrong payouts. Affiliates lose trust. Creators leave. Networks lose partners. The entire ecosystem erodes when attribution data cannot be verified.
